Mr. Archil Sikharulidze

Mr. Archil Sikharulidze is a PhD Candidate in Social Sciences at Georgian Instite of Public Affairs and an Invited Lecturer at Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University. Also, he is a Software Developer with in-depth knowledge of the following programming languages: C/C++, Java, JavaScript, HTML & CSS. As a young scholar-researcher he is focused on Russian Studies, democratization and regime change in the post-Soviet space, political aspects of Islam in Georgia and Higher Education System reform. He extensively writes for various prominent Russian and English-language analytical platforms such as OpenDemocracy, RIAC, New Eastern Europe, Caucasus Watch and etc.
